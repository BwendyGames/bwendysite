Who am I?

My dad bought me Game Maker Studio 2 when I was around 12 years old. It's safe to say that was a mistake, and, unfortunately; I still am making video games. I go by Bwendy online, a nickname from around the same time, which is sort of a crude mispronunciation of about half of my name. I am, at any given point, working on a couple of concepts for books or short stories.

I'm a big fan of fantasy and it's many offshoots, I also play D&D, and take inspirations from many pieces of media like Adventure Time, the works of Brandon Sanderson, Tolkien, and more.
